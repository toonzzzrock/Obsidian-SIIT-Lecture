# Content Distribution Network (CDN)

- CDN: system of distributed servers that deliver web content to a user based on **geographic** location of user, origin of webpage and content delivery server
- CDN nodes: servers that store cached content closer to end users
- CDN nodes are distributed across different geographic locations to ensure faster content delivery and reduced latency for users

## Private CDN 
- A **Private CDN** is built and operated by a single company to deliver its own content exclusively. Instead of paying an outside service, the company places its own servers deep within ISP networks to gain complete control over how its data is handled.

##  Public CDN
- A **Public CDN** is a service provided by a third-party company that rents out its distributed infrastructure to many clients.
