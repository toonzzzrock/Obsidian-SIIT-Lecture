# Revenue

Increases [[Owner's Equity]]
- **Sales Revenue** - sale of merchandise
either cash sales or credit sales.
- **Service Fee Revenue** - performance of
services to clients (medical, accounting).
- **Commission Revenue** - performance of
service based on successful completion of
business transactions (brokers, agents).
- **Interest Revenue** - from notes receivable
or loan.
