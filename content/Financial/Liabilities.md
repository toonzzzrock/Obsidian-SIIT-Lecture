# Liabilities

- **Current Liabilities:** obligations due within 1 year
    - **Notes Payable (N/P)** - promissory notes to creditors to pay certain amount of money usually with interest.
    - **Accounts Payable (A/P)** - creditors arising out of credit purchases.
    - **Unearned Revenue** - cash received in advance before services are rendered or goods are delivered.
    - **Other Payables** - expenses which are already due but not yet paid during accounting period (salaries payable, interest payable, and utilities payable).
- **Non-Current Liabilities**: obligations due beyond 1 year
    - **Mortgage Payable** - long-term loan secured by a mortgage on real estate.
    - **Bonds Payable** - long-term debt securities issued to investors for a period longer than 1 year.
    - **Long-term Notes Payable** - promissory notes to creditors to pay certain amount of money usually with interest.
