# Owner's Equity

- **Owner's Capital**  - opened with owner’s initial investment in business. Balance at beginning of period shows result of additional investment, net income or loss, and owner’s withdrawal.
- **Owners’ Withdrawal** - payment of cash or other assets for owner’s personal expenses. Owner’s withdrawal reduces owner’s equity.
